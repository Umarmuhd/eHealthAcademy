Easy Roasted chicken

  • 1 Onion & 6 Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon powder
  • Pinch of Salt & Seasoning
  • small oil/water e.t.c

Easy roasted chicken tasted great

Source: KN-pearl
  • Use a blender to blend onion/pepper/ginger/garlic to make a mixture and pour on a large bowl, add seasoning/soy sauce/cinnamon powder and turn altogether. (marination)
  • Wash chicken with clean water and remove all it yucky things from its stomach and wash again.
  • Put the chicken in a bowl and pour the mixture on it, brush both inside/out of chicken make sure you rob all of it and marinate for some time which might be hour or more.
  • spread foil paper on an oven tray, place chicken on slice some lemon and pour remaining mixture on then cover it and roast for almost an hour.
  • check to see if its done and serve enjoy.

Italian Homemade Farfalle pasta

Source: KN-pearl
  • First, we wash our hands, use a flat surface or a bowl, and pour flour (an amount you want to make) make a hole in the middle and pour egg, sprinkle some salt (optional), and then water. Knead the dough for some time until it all corporated together, close, and leave for some time.
    Note: make sure the dough is not sticking on your hands if it is, then add a little flour, wet your hands and knead the dough again so that all come in together which will ‘ll not stick on your hands then keep aside.
  • On a flat surface or you can just use a rolling pin to roll the dough, cut it into a smaller rectangular shape, use your fingers to pinch the middle together so that it’ll give the butterfly shape. Pour your pasta on a pot, add water and boil it until it becomes soft.
  • farfalle pasta sauce
    Wash and cut cabbage/carrot/onion/pepper/green pepper, on a low flame drizzle some oil on the pan and pour all your chopped ingredients inside and stir for 5 minutes,add some spice until it is done.
  • Pour sauce on a plate, add pasta on top and serve.

Fried rice

Ingridients Details Step-by-step procedure
  • Cooked, chilled rice
  • Butter, Eggs, Garlic
  • Toasted sesame oil and oyster sauce
  • Pinch of Salt & Seasoning
  • Carrots, onions, green onions and peas
  • small oil/water e.t.c

Delicious Fried rice

Source: Gimme-some-oven
  • Scramble your eggs: Using a small pat of butter, scramble some eggs in a large sauté pan, breaking them into small pieces as you go. Then transfer the eggs to a separate plate, and set aside.
  • Sauté your veggies and garlic: Return your sauté pan to the heat, and sauté the onions, carrots, peas and garlic until soft and cooked through.
  • Stir fry rice: Then turn the burner to high heat. Scooch the veggies over to one side of the pan, melt the remaining butter in the other half, and add the chilled rice, soy sauce, and oyster sauce (if using). Then stir to combine with the veggies and continue sautéing the rice, stirring every 15-20 seconds or so for 3 minutes, or until you notice the rice and veggies starting to brown slightly.
  • Remove pan from heat. And stir in your green onions, sesame oil, and scrambled eggs.
  • Taste and season. Give the rice a taste, and season with salt and pepper, plus any extra soy sauce or sesame oil if needed.
  • Serve! Then dish it up and serve nice and warm!
